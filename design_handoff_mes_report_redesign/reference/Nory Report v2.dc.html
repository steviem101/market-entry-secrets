11 · SETUP & COMPLIANCE — THE IRELAND→AUSTRALIA CHECKLIST
The Australia–Ireland double tax treaty streamlines the path, but early planning avoids the slow steps. The structural requirements first, then tax, people and banking.
REQUIREMENTWHAT THE RESEARCH SAYSPRACTICAL NOTE
Resident director
ASIC · BLOCKING At least one director must be "ordinarily resident" in Australia; a short-term visa holder does not qualify Hire a local manager or engage a nominee director; every director needs a Director ID — the paper process from Ireland takes 4–6 weeks, so start first
Tax & GST
ATO · ● GST registration mandatory over A$75k turnover (10% on sales); corporate rate 30% / 25% base-rate vs Ireland's 15% Double tax treaty prevents double taxation; dividend withholding generally capped at 15%; R&DTI returns 43.5% on AU-conducted R&D
Privacy & data
PRIVACY ACT 1988 · ● NDB scheme applies once turnover exceeds A$3M; payroll data is high-sensitivity Document data flows, draft a DPA, and establish breach procedures with the OAIC before the first enterprise deal
People & banking
FAIR WORK · ● NES + industry awards set minimum pay; 482 TSS is the primary relocation visa (2–4 years, sponsored employer) An EOR (e.g. Deel) validates the market before full incorporation; digital-first banking (Airwallex, Wise Business) opens faster than the Big Four
