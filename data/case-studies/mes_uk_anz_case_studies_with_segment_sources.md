## Case Study 1: Revolut

### Company Overview
Revolut was founded in London in 2015 by Nikolay Storonsky and Vlad Yatsenko to reduce the cost and friction of international spending and money movement, beginning with a prepaid travel card and interbank FX model before expanding into broader financial services.[web:153]

**Sources**
- [Revolut Australia newsroom and milestones](https://www.revolut.com/en-AU/news/)
- [Invest Victoria on Revolut choosing Melbourne](https://www.invest.vic.gov.au/news-events/news/2020/revolut-launches-in-australia)
- [ASIC professional registers / AFSL search](https://connectonline.asic.gov.au/)

### Why ANZ?
Australia offered Revolut an English-speaking market with high smartphone adoption, frequent international travel and remittance use, and a concentrated banking sector where pricing opacity created room for a challenger proposition.[web:153]

**Sources**
- [Invest Victoria on market rationale and launch support](https://www.invest.vic.gov.au/news-events/news/2020/revolut-launches-in-australia)
- [RFI Global / Australian banking and digital adoption coverage](https://www.rfigroup.com/)
- [Reserve Bank of Australia payments statistics](https://www.rba.gov.au/statistics/)

### Entry Journey
Revolut established its Australian base in Melbourne, secured regulatory approval before public launch, then expanded from travel and FX into crypto, equity trading, rewards, and business products as it deepened local market fit.[web:153]

**Sources**
- [Invest Victoria on Revolut's Melbourne launch](https://www.invest.vic.gov.au/news-events/news/2020/revolut-launches-in-australia)
- [Revolut Australia product and customer announcements](https://www.revolut.com/en-AU/news/)
- [ASIC registers](https://connectonline.asic.gov.au/)

### Key Outcomes
Revolut has grown from a very small founding team in Australia into a scaled local operation with a seven-figure customer base and a much broader product suite, making it one of the clearest UK fintech expansion stories for MES.[web:153]

**Sources**
- [Revolut Australia newsroom](https://www.revolut.com/en-AU/news/)
- [Industry coverage on Australian growth](https://www.afr.com/companies/financial-services)
- [Invest Victoria profile](https://www.invest.vic.gov.au/news-events/news/2020/revolut-launches-in-australia)
